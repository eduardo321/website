What is Audacity you may be wondering? Basically Audacity is a software program that allows you to create your own sound or use a source which already contains sound for you to edit. It also allows you to manipulate sound. One of the ways you can manipulate sound is by adding effects to whatever sound you like. It is very easy to add effects to sound. One of the many things that you can do is create your own tone. You do this by going on to generate and then you click on tone.

Another way to add sound through Audacity, is by simply recording you own voice by pressing the record button and then you can make any sound you like. When clicking on tone you will get to decide you own frequency and the duration of that sound. The effect of increasing the frequency is that you increase the pitch, so you will make the sound higher. This means that frequency and pitch are linked between each other. You press stop when you have finished and if you want to hear it back then you simply press play.

You can zoom in on any sound that you create. Zooming in is a great way of seeing the waves really clearly.
