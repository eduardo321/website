decibels (dbs) - - A decibel measures the intensity of a particular sound. Here are a few examples of decibels.

  1. Someone whispering is - 15db
  2. A normal conversation is - 60db
  3. A lanmower is 90 - db
  4. A car beep is 110 - db

As you know distance effects sounds, such as the further you go from a sound the quieter it becomes, but the closer you get the louder it becomes.
